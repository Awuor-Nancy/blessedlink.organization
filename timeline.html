No.11

2013

We relocated to Kenya of more zeal thank knowledge. We were overjoyed with the fact that God was going to use us, that our timing was off. We began feeding street children bananas, biscuits(cookies) and non-refrigerated lost lasting milk.

No.12

2007

Relocated to Cary, North Carolina We joined Grace Bible Fellowship church a few months after arriving with our then 100 year old great Aunt . We both began our careers and allowed our Christ like behaviors to be a witness of His love. This is where we began to hear our call to missions in Kenya after several trail and error sessions in the US.

We sat under the excellent biblical teaching of Dr. Pastor Norman Peart. This was a time of refreshing, soul searching, repenting and being very still before God. We heard God ask the question, like Isaiah , “ who will go? We did not answer for sometime, but finally we would answer and say send us Lord.
